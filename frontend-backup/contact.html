Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I expect a response?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our support number for immediate assistance.

Do you offer customer support on weekends?

Our email support team monitors messages during limited hours on weekends. For premium customers, we offer 24/7 support via our dedicated support portal.

How do I request a refund?

Refund requests should be submitted via email to billing@healthyseo.com with your account details and reason for the refund. Please review our refund policy in our Terms of Service for eligibility criteria.

Can I schedule a consultation call?

Yes! We offer free 30-minute consultation calls for potential customers. Please use our contact form to request a consultation and mention your preferred date and time.
