About Healthy SEO

Our Mission

At Healthy SEO, our mission is to provide accessible, comprehensive website analysis and optimization tools to businesses of all sizes. We believe that understanding your website's health should be simple, actionable, and affordable.

Founded in 2023, Healthy SEO has grown from a simple SEO checker to a comprehensive website health platform used by thousands of businesses worldwide to improve their online presence.

What We Do

Healthy SEO provides a complete suite of tools to analyze and improve your website's:

  • SEO Performance - Understand how search engines see your site and identify optimization opportunities
  • Page Speed - Analyze loading times and performance metrics that affect user experience and rankings
  • Security - Identify potential security vulnerabilities and implement protective measures
  • Accessibility - Ensure your website is accessible to all users, including those with disabilities
  • Mobile Optimization - Check that your website performs well on all devices
  • Content Quality - Analyze your content for readability, relevance, and engagement potential
